ITEM DESCRIPTIONS

------------------------------------------------------------
Bomb

As an action, you can light the bomb and throw it at a point up to 60 feet away.

Each creature within 5 feet of that point must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw.
On a failure: 3d6 fire damage.
On a success: No damage unless otherwise determined by the DM.

------------------------------------------------------------
Gunpowder

Gunpowder is used to fire bullets or create bombs.
It is sold in:
- Small wooden kegs
- Water-resistant powder horns

If a full container is set on fire:

Powder Horn Explosion
Damage: 3d6 fire
Radius: 10 feet
Save: DC 12 Dexterity for half damage

Keg Explosion
Damage: 7d6 fire
Radius: 10 feet
Save: DC 12 Dexterity for half damage

If one ounce of gunpowder is ignited:
- Burns for 1 round
- Bright light: 30-foot radius
- Dim light: Additional 30 feet

------------------------------------------------------------
Dynamite

As an action, you can light a stick of dynamite and throw it up to 60 feet.

Each creature within 5 feet must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw.
On a failure: 3d6 bludgeoning damage.
On a success: Half damage.

Multiple Sticks
Each additional stick:
- +1d6 damage (maximum 10d6 total)
- +5 feet burst radius (maximum 20 feet radius)

Timed Fuse
Dynamite can be set to explode in 1 to 6 rounds.
Roll initiative for the dynamite.
It explodes on its initiative after the set number of rounds.

------------------------------------------------------------
Grenades

As an action, you can throw a grenade up to 60 feet.
With a grenade launcher, range increases to 120 feet.

Fragmentation Grenade
Radius: 20 feet
Save: DC 15 Dexterity
Failure: 5d6 piercing damage
Success: Half damage

Smoke Grenade
One round after landing:
- Creates heavily obscured area
- 20-foot radius

Wind Effects:
- Moderate wind (10+ mph): disperses in 4 rounds
- Strong wind (20+ mph): disperses in 1 round
