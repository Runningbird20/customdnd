WEAPONS

Your class grants proficiency in certain weapons. Your weapon choice and your ability to wield it effectively can mean the difference between life and death.

Weapons are divided into:
- Melee Weapons (used within 5 feet)
- Ranged Weapons (used at a distance)

============================================================
WEAPON PROPERTIES (SUMMARY)

Ammunition
Requires ammunition to make ranged attacks. Half recoverable after battle.

Finesse
Use Strength or Dexterity for attack and damage.

Heavy
Small or Tiny creatures have disadvantage.

Light
Ideal for two-weapon fighting.

Loading
Only one piece of ammunition per action.

Range
Two numbers: normal range / maximum range. Disadvantage beyond normal range.

Reach
Adds 5 feet to attack reach.

Special
Weapon has unique rules.

Thrown
Can be thrown for ranged attacks.

Two-Handed
Requires two hands when attacking.

Versatile
Can be used one-handed or two-handed.

============================================================
IMPROVISED WEAPONS

Objects used as weapons deal 1d4 damage if not similar to an actual weapon.
Thrown improvised weapons: 20 ft normal, 60 ft long range.

============================================================
SILVERED WEAPONS

Cost: 100 gp to silver one weapon or 10 pieces of ammunition.

============================================================
SPECIAL WEAPONS RULES

Lance
Disadvantage against targets within 5 feet.
Requires two hands when not mounted.

Net
Restrains Large or smaller creatures.
DC 10 Strength check to escape.
5 slashing damage (AC 10) destroys net.
Only one attack per action with a net.

Hoopak
Can be used as melee or ranged weapon.
Uses sling bullets when ranged.
Bonus attack deals 1d4 damage.

Double-Bladed Scimitar
After attacking, can use bonus action to deal 1d4 slashing damage.
